The Physical Commodity Trading System™ — 12-Month Private System

Most commodity traders don't have a deal flow problem. They have a system problem.

The market has deals. There are buyers who need product and sellers who need offtakers every single day. The problem is that without a system, you only access a fraction of what's available — and that fraction depends on who you happen to know, who picks up the phone, and whether your one or two reliable counterparties are active this month.

When they're not, your pipeline stops. You spend weeks chasing leads that go nowhere. You find a deal worth doing but can't finance it. You close one good trade and spend the next month trying to repeat it with no clear idea of how you did it the first time.

That's not bad luck. That's what trading without a system looks like.

12 months from now, your pipeline runs on a system — not on luck, timing, or who happens to pick up the phone.
